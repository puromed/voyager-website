Hot Road

The legacy of the Voyager Hot Road traces back to the late 1960s, a time of automotive revolution and the burgeoning of a new era in performance cars. Visionary automotive engineer, Arfan Haziq, spearheaded a groundbreaking project aimed at crafting a vehicle that transcended boundaries, blending raw power with unparalleled design innovation. Voyager Hot Road set out to create the perfect sports car, and legend has it that the brand's founder offered engineer and designer Puteri Hazimah a bonus for each additional power output that he could get with the new 3.5-liter V12 engine mounted 60° with a twin overhead, able to supply 320 CV on the 350 GT. This is how the Voyager Hot Road was born.
