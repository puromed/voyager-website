2015

VOYAGER

Welcome to Voyager, where driving becomes an art, and every journey is a celebration of the extraordinary. Voyager, a visionary automotive company, was founded to redefine luxury in the automobile industry. Elevate your expectations, as we redefine the very essence of luxury on the open road.

2017

SPECTRE

Responding to the growing demand for eco-friendly options, Voyager launched the Spectre in 2017. This model featured a state-of-the-art hybrid powertrain, providing a seamless performance and environmental consciousness blend. The X1 Hybrid set a new standard for sustainable luxury.

2019

HOT ROAD

Expanding their lineup, Voyager introduced the Hot Road in 2019. This luxury Hot Road boasted a spacious interior, advanced safety features, and a powerful engine. The Hot Road quickly became a favorite among affluent families and adventure enthusiasts.

2022

PATH FINDER

In a bold move towards a greener future, Voyager unveiled the Path Finder in 2022. This all-electric luxury car was equipped with cutting-edge battery technology, offering an impressive range and rapid charging capabilities. The X3 Electric marked Voyagers' commitment to sustainable innovation.

2024

XS HYPERCAR

To celebrate our achievements over the years, Voyager will introduce the XS Hypercar in 2024. This limited edition masterpiece combines unparalleled performance with stunning design. With a top speed that pushes the boundaries of what is thought possible, the XS Hypercar is solid Voyagers position as a leader in the luxury automotive industry.
